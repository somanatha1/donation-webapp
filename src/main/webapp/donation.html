Our Mission

To bring positive change through food distribution, education support, healthcare assistance, and community development.

Founded on the principles of empathy and service, we believe that even the smallest acts of kindness can create a ripple effect of change. With the support of our generous donors and passionate volunteers, we have been able to impact countless lives.

At Jagannath Anugrah Trust, transparency and accountability are at the core of everything we do. Every donation goes directly into uplifting communities and empowering individuals.
